my journey with nanos

So I have to teach myself on nano materials and composites and even physics to further my research on nano crystals and mircospheres, which has trained my self-study ability. Also I made my personal website mostly by myself with about one hour learning basic knowledge, and I think it is a strong proof of my ability. But the most important thing is interest. I am applying for PhD for this area because I am interested, and I do believe this passion can make myself a better person and make my life more colorful, just like quantum dots!
